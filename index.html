Privacy Policy — Nib

Effective date: June 4, 2026

What this extension does

Nib lets you select text in an editable field on a website and rewrite it using AI. It activates only when you select text and click "Rephrase."

What we process

When you click "Rephrase," the text you selected is sent to our backend endpoint (an Azure Function we operate), which forwards it to Microsoft Azure OpenAI to generate a rewritten version that is returned to you. We process only the text you explicitly select for rephrasing.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not store or log the text you send. It is processed in memory to fulfill your request and then discarded.
  • We do not require accounts or collect any identifying information.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
  • We do not sell or share your data with anyone, for any purpose other than generating your rephrased text.

Third-party processing

Text is processed by Microsoft Azure OpenAI Service to produce the rewrite, acting as our service provider. Microsoft does not use Azure OpenAI input/output to train its models. See Microsoft's data, privacy, and security documentation for the Azure OpenAI Service for details.

Data retention

None. We keep no copy of your selected text or the AI output.

Security

Your selected text is transmitted over HTTPS. The AI provider's API key is held only on our server (the Azure Function) and is never included in the extension.

Permissions

The extension runs on the websites where you write so it can detect your text selection and show its interface. It reads only the text you actively select — never page content at large, and never on its own.

Children

This extension is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date above will change accordingly.
